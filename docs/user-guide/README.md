## Getting Started

### For Employees

1. **Login to the system** using your credentials
2. **Navigate to Leave Management** section
3. **Apply for leave** by filling the leave request form
4. **Track your leave requests** in the dashboard
5. **View leave balance** and history

### For Managers

1. **Login to the system** using your credentials
2. **View pending approvals** in the dashboard
3. **Review leave requests** from your team
4. **Approve or reject** leave requests
5. **View team leave calendar** and reports

### For HR

1. **Login to the system** using your credentials
2. **Access leave reports** and analytics
3. **Manage leave policies** and configurations
4. **View organization-wide leave statistics**
5. **Export reports** for analysis

## Features

### Leave Application
- Apply for different types of leave

- Add reason for leave
- View leave balance before applying

### Leave Approval
- Review leave requests
- Approve or reject with comments
- View leave calendar
- Track team attendance

### Reports
- View leave statistics
- Export reports
- Analyze leave patterns
- Generate custom reports

## FAQ

### How do I apply for leave?
Navigate to Leave Management > Apply Leave, fill in the form, and submit.

### Can I cancel a leave request?
Yes, you can cancel a leave request if it's still pending approval.

### How do I check my leave balance?
Your leave balance is displayed on the dashboard and in the Leave Management section.

### What if my leave request is rejected?
You will receive a notification with the reason. You can apply again with updated information.

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot login
- Check your credentials
- Contact system administrator
- Reset password if needed

### Leave request not showing
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Contact support if issue persists
